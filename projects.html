Ambassador Hotel - Amarillo, TX

Asset Summary

First Capital Builders has been retained to complete a full remodel of the Ambassador Hotel Amarillo in preparation for its affiliation with Wyndham Hotels and Resorts. The Ambassador is a 263-unit ten-story hotel and our remodel will encompass repair to the exterior of the property, mechanical systems, all 263 rooms, the lobby and common guest areas, as well as construction of the new Amarillo Pour House, a full service bar and restaurant. This $4,000,000 renovation will be complete by late 2014.

Tonopalo - Lake Tahoe, California

Highlights

The Tonopalo Resort Community is fractional resort style development built in 2004 along Lake Tahoe’s north shore in Tahoe Vista, CA. This mountain retreat consists of nineteen furnished units, a clubhouse, swimming pool, and spa located on 270’ of sandy beach with expansive views of the Sierra Nevada mountains and Lake Tahoe. The three and four bedroom units feature extensive living and dining areas, spectacular fireplaces, double master bedrooms, gourmet kitchens, and private patios, all with lake views.
